QR's Rollingstock¶

QR currently operates a fleet of :

  • 75 NGRs (701-775)[1]
  • 78 SMUs (201-296)[2]:
    • 12 SMU200s (201-212)
    • 30 SMU220s (221-250)
    • 36 SMU260s (261-296)
  • 48 IMUs (101-188)[2]:
    • 10 IMU100s (101-110)
    • 4 IMU120s (121-124)
    • 28 IMU160s (161-188)
  • ~30 EMUs (01-88 exc. 01-24, 30-38, 42, 48, 52, 54, 58, 60-79, and 80) [3]

QR also previously operated 20 ICE-class carriages in 4-6 car formations (all withdrawn by 2021)[4] and are currently building 65 new six-car "QTMP" trains that are all planned to enter service before 2032[5].
